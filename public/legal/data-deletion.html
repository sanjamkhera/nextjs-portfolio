Kitty Diary

Data Deletion

Last updated: June 2025

If you wish to delete your account and all associated data from Kitty Diary, please email us at notify@seawallsoftware.ca with the subject "Delete My Data" and include the email address you used to sign up. We will process your request within 30 days.

How to Identify Yourself

To ensure we can locate your data, please provide the following information in your email:

  • Your full name
  • The email address associated with your account
  • Any additional information that may help us identify your account (e.g., user ID, if available)

What Happens Next

Once we receive your request, we will verify your identity and proceed with the deletion of your account and associated data. You will receive a confirmation email once the process is complete.
