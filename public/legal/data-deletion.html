Kitty Diary

Data Deletion

Last updated: June 2025

If you wish to delete your account and all associated data from Kitty Diary, please follow these steps:

1. Submit Your Deletion Request

Email us at notify@seawallsoftware.ca with:

  • Subject line: "Delete My Data"
  • Your full name
  • The email address associated with your account
  • Any additional information that may help us identify your account (e.g., user ID, if available)

2. Confirmation and Tracking

After submitting your request, you will receive:

  • A confirmation email with your unique deletion request ID
  • A link to track the status of your deletion request
Note: Your deletion request ID will be in the format: KD-XXXX-XXXX (e.g., KD-1234-5678)

3. What Happens Next

We will process your request within 30 days. You can track the status of your request using the link provided in the confirmation email. The process includes:

  • Verification of your identity
  • Deletion of your account and associated data
  • Confirmation email once the process is complete

4. Status Check

To check the status of your deletion request, use the link provided in your confirmation email or visit:

https://seawallsoftware.ca/legal/data-deletion-status.html

You will need your deletion request ID to check the status.

This data deletion policy was last updated on June 2025.
