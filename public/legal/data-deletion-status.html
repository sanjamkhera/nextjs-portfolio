About Data Deletion

When you submit a data deletion request, we process it within 30 days. The status of your request will be one of the following:

  • Received: We have received your request and are processing it
  • In Progress: We are currently processing your deletion request
  • Completed: Your data has been successfully deleted
  • Failed: We encountered an issue processing your request (you will be notified)

If you haven't received a deletion request ID, please submit your deletion request first by emailing us at notify@seawallsoftware.ca.
